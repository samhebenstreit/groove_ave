Who We Are

The Band

Groove Ave is an eight-piece soul and funk collective born out of a shared love for the music that moves you — not just your feet, but something deeper. Drawing from the rich traditions of 70s soul, classic R&B, and hard-hitting funk, the band has built a reputation for electrifying live performances that leave audiences wanting more.

From intimate venues to festival stages, Groove Ave brings warmth, precision, and an undeniable groove to every show. With soaring vocal harmonies, a blazing horn section, searing guitar lines, and a rhythm section that locks in tight, this is music that gets under your skin and stays there.
